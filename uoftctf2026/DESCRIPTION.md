## UofTCTF 2026

UofTCTF 2026 is the annual capture-the-flag competition organized by the UofTCTF team. This year’s event will take place online from January 9, 2026, at 7:00 PM EST to January 11, 2026, at 7:00 PM EST. It will be a jeopardy-style CTF with classic categories like web, pwn, crypto, rev, forensics, and misc.
